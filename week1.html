Website Development Inspiration:

My two biggest recent inspirations are the portfolios of Alexander Vilinskyy and Patrick David. Both are web developers in their own regard. When it comes to creating my own work, I struggle with balancing maximalism and minimalism. On the outside, I present myself as very minimal, but in actuality, I have a lot of thoughts going on, and that really reflects in my work.

Alexandars portfolio has a very basic color scheme, which I enjoy, while Patrick Davids work is fun. It's animated, it's silly, but still structured.
